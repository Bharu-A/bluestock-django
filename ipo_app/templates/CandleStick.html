Candlestick Charts & Patterns

Candlestick patterns are a type of financial chart that graphically displays the high, low, open, and close prices of a security for a specific period.

1. Bullish Engulfing

A small red candle followed by a large green candle engulfing it — signals reversal upward.

2. Bearish Engulfing

Appears at top of uptrend — green candle then a red one fully engulfing it — signals reversal down.

3. Hammer

Looks like a hammer — small body on top, long lower wick. Bullish sign.

4. Shooting Star

Inverted hammer after uptrend — bearish reversal signal.
